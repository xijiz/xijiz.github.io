Biography

I am a second-year master student of computer science at Zhejiang University. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yin Zhang. My research interests include natural language processing, affective computing, deep learning, and search ranking. Now, I dedicate to information extraction, especially named entity recognition.

I have received my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Northeast Electric Power University in 2018. During my master, I spent internships at SkyMobi in 2019 and Microsoft in 2020.
